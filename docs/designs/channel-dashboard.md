Generated by /plan-ceo-review on 2026-03-25

## Vision

### 10x Check
The 10x version is the nervous system of HyperClaw — not just a feed widget, but an event correlation engine with smart watchers, pattern matching, time-travel scroll, cross-device aggregation, and inline action buttons that turn passive monitoring into active system control. The dashboard becomes a living timeline of your AI system where events flow in with context-rich detail, errors rise with urgency, and you can respond inline without switching widgets.

### Platonic Ideal
You open HyperClaw and events flow in with subtle animations. Colors tell you the category at a glance. You click an agent completion — it expands to show the full response, tools called, tokens used. A cron failure arrives with a red pulse. You click it, see the error, hit "Rerun." When you scroll up to read something, new events stack silently below — a "3 new events" pill appears. The feed remembers your scroll position. It feels like the system is talking to you — ambient awareness without effort.
